<h2><a href="/graflens/">GrafLens</a></h2>

<p><strong>A free, open-source native iOS viewer for Grafana dashboards.</strong></p>

<p>Browse dashboards, monitor alerts, manage annotations, create snapshots, and pin panels as home screen widgets — all from your iPhone or iPad. No ads, no tracking, fully open source.</p>

<ul>
    <li>Connect to any self-hosted or cloud Grafana instance</li>
    <li>Home screen widgets for any panel</li>
    <li>Siri Shortcuts integration</li>
    <li>Multiple server connections</li>
</ul>

<h2><a href="/solacoffea/">Sola Coffea</a></h2>

<p><strong>A native iOS app for coffee obsessives.</strong></p>

<p>Catalog coffees, log brews with method-specific parameters, track your taste profile across origins and roasters, discover shops nearby, and share your coffee journey with a community of enthusiasts.</p>

<ul>
    <li>Coffee library with tasting notes, ratings, and photos</li>
    <li>Brew logging for every method — espresso, pour-over, AeroPress, and more</li>
    <li>Insights and analytics that grow with your library</li>
    <li>Origin map and Coffee DNA flavor profile</li>
    <li>Shop discovery with Bean Board for roasters and cafes</li>
    <li>Social feed, Coffee Circles, and community reviews</li>
    <li>iCloud sync across all your devices</li>
</ul>
