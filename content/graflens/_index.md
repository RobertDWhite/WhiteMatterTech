<h1>GrafLens</h1>

<p><strong>A free, open-source native iOS viewer for Grafana dashboards.</strong></p>

<p>GrafLens lets you browse Grafana dashboards, monitor alerts, manage annotations, and more — all from your iPhone or iPad.</p>

<h2>Features</h2>

<ul>
    <li>Connect to any Grafana instance via service account token</li>
    <li>Browse dashboards organized by folder with full-text search</li>
    <li>View panels with configurable time ranges (5m to 30d)</li>
    <li>Monitor and silence firing alerts</li>
    <li>Create annotations to mark events and incidents</li>
    <li>Generate shareable dashboard snapshots</li>
    <li>Manage folders and playlists</li>
    <li>Native iPad sidebar layout</li>
    <li>Home screen widgets for any panel</li>
    <li>Siri Shortcuts integration</li>
    <li>Dark and light mode support</li>
    <li>Multiple server connections</li>
    <li>No ads, no tracking, fully open source</li>
</ul>
