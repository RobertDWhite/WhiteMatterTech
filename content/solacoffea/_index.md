<h1>Sola Coffea</h1>

<p><strong>A native iOS app for coffee obsessives.</strong></p>

<p>Sola Coffea is built for people who approach coffee with intention — whether you are cataloguing single-origin beans, calibrating espresso extractions, or discovering new shops while traveling. It started as a personal logging tool and grew into a comprehensive platform covering brew analytics, shop discovery, and a community of enthusiasts.</p>

<p><a href="https://apps.apple.com/us/app/sola-coffea/id6737065958"><strong>Download on the App Store</strong></a></p>

<h2>Features</h2>

<ul>
    <li>Coffee library with roaster, origin, process, variety, tasting notes, ratings, and photos</li>
    <li>Brew logging with method-specific parameters — grind, temperature, dose, yield, TDS, and extraction yield for espresso</li>
    <li>Insights and analytics that surface flavor preferences, rating trends, and brewing patterns</li>
    <li>Origin map showing every coffee-producing region you have explored</li>
    <li>Coffee DNA: a personalized taste profile derived from your ratings and notes</li>
    <li>Shop discovery with visit logging and Google Maps import</li>
    <li>Bean Board for roasters and cafes to publish daily menus and brew guides</li>
    <li>Social feed, Coffee Circles, and community reviews</li>
    <li>23 step-by-step brew guides with integrated timers</li>
    <li>iCloud sync across iPhone and iPad</li>
</ul>

<h2>Pricing</h2>

<p>Sola Coffea is free to download. Core tracking features are included at no cost. A Pro subscription unlocks advanced insights, the brew lab, data export, shop intelligence, and additional capabilities. Monthly, annual, and lifetime options are available, along with a business tier for roasters and cafe proprietors.</p>
